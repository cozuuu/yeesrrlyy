CHAPTER 2: Before Us

It all started with a simple question under a profile on a LeoMatch Bot: "Frontend or backend?"

I slipped into your private chat and answered, "Currently, I'm learning frontend." That was our very first talk online.

Two days later, we made it official. To be honest, we both secretly thought it might be a short-term thing... we didn't know yet how strong we would become. But we were so wrong. Here we are, 8 months later, proving how deep this love runs.

We used to share every Sunday and every free evening together. Now, the distance has temporarily changed our scenery, but it hasn't changed a single thing about us. We are waiting for the day we get to meet again.

"Distance never changed where home was."
